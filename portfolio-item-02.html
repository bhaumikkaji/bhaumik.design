Microsoft Teams Polls

Microsoft Teams is the hub of communication and productivity for big and small organizations around the world. Asking for an opinion or collating feedback from your team, is an essential need in a chat or a meeting setup. Part of this project we wanted to build productivity mini-apps like Polls, Checklist and Survey inbuilt into Microsoft Teams. Currently these capabilities exist as part of 3rd party apps, which is at risk to leaking confidential data out of the organization.

ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITY

Lead designer for designing experiences for Survey, Checklist and Polls in Microsoft Teams.

IMPACT

Business: Increase in the usage of 1st party apps in Microsoft Teams. No risk of leaking data outside the organization.

Product: Polls has become integral to teams, and is now part of Microsoft Forms integration.

Users: Now users can ask questions, take a poll and collect data in a chat or a meeting with ease.

Design system to set up coherent experience for 1st party apps on Web and Mobile apps within Microsoft.

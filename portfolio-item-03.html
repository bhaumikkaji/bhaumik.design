User Experience Designer

Microsoft Kaizala Actions

Microsoft Kaizala is a productivity chat app for extended workforce and front-line workers in large and medium organizations. Kaizala has built in mini apps that help organizations and teams be productive on chat. As a lead designer on this Mini apps (actions) framework, I got a chance to design directly experiences for Chat, Poll, Survey, Job, Location Apps, Payments within the app.

ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITY

Lead designer to set up a framework for designing 1st and 3rd party mini app experiences within Kaizala app.

IMPACT

End user: Easy and familiar way to work and chat.

Business: Increase in product usage and new users joining the platform.

Product: A experimental app to test the needs of emerging markets for Microsoft.

Survey app was one significant app that I got to design from scratch, user-test and research for.

The First product from Microsoft IDC to be built in India and be part of the Office(M365) ecosystem.

Lot of the capabilities that I got to design and build are not part of Microsoft Team and services used for Enterprise customers.

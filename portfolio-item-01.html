Microsoft Stream Mobile

Microsoft Stream, is an enterprise video platform. Part of the Microsoft 365 subscription. Part of this project, I am the lead designer for mobile experiences. Microsoft Stream is an integrated service that enables enterprise video jobs in multiple apps such as Microsoft Teams, Microsoft Office, Microsoft OneDrive and more.

This involved me to design the video player/consumption experience that empowers users in all these apps to perform those video jobs. This strategy takes care of the breath of users who want to do video consumption in these apps.

There is also a Microsoft Stream Mobile App that caters to the depth. This project will showcase the research and design led by me for the Stream App and the Stream video player experiences on Android and iOS platforms.

ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITY

Project 1: Designing a high value control (HVC) for performing video consumption jobs in M365 users. Video Player.

Project 2: Re-Design the Stream Mobile App to address the Discovery, Consumption, Curation and Creation jobs.

Shipping the MLP(Most loveable product) with focus on quality and craft.

Design research to understand what are the enterprise video jobs that we need to design for.

Close collaboration with partner teams to design and ship the experience in their apps.

Plan a product strategy on mobile

Lead other designers in the team who work on same space

WHY HIRE?

Design Leadership

I aspire to be a Design leader and help organizations leverage design discipline better. Microsoft has helped me understand the role of design in a company Triad (Engg.-PM-Design). I want to lead my own team to help inc. the design impact and nurture young talent in the industry.

Interaction Design

I have more than 6+ years in Mobile design. I love to design with Figma and create rich prototypes on ProtoPie. I am strong advocate of prototyping first approach to design. I also champion to create design system to help designers and developers scale.

Motion Design

I love to storytell and find any excuse to create a showcase video for my projects. I use motion to make the rich user experience and add user delight with microinteractions. I love to work with After effects and Lottie to ship animations to live code.

Design Research

Fail fast, succeed faster. In my design process, the role of user testing and feedback is integral. I am strong advocator of Jobs to be done framework. From the requirement stage, to design and testing during shipping, I have a great experience in leading design research to succeed.
